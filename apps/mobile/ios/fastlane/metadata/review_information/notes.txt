No account, phone number, or email is required to use Speakeasy — please do
not request demo credentials.

Getting in:
1. Launch the app and tap "Open the door."
2. The app generates an anonymous cryptographic identity on-device and asks
   you to pick a handle (e.g. @reviewer) and a face/avatar. That's the whole
   sign-up — nothing is sent off the device about who you are.

Testing messaging + calls (needs two installs):
3. Install on a second device (or a second TestFlight install). On each,
   open the handle screen to see your @handle + QR code.
4. On device A, add device B by scanning B's QR (or pasting B's handle), then
   start a chat or a Private Call.
5. All messages and calls are end-to-end encrypted (Signal Protocol); calls
   additionally apply a voice-masking filter. The server only ever sees
   ciphertext.

Notes:
- Private Calls require microphone permission; messaging needs no special
  permissions.
- There is no central directory — you can only reach someone whose handle or
  QR you already have, by design.
