Hello, and thank you for the detailed review.

We've made the following changes to address both guidelines.

**Guideline 1.1 — Objectionable Content (marketing/metadata)**

We've revised the app's description, subtitle, keywords, and promotional text to
remove the terms that prompted this flag. Specifically, we removed "anonymous"
and references to voice masking from all marketing metadata, and clarified that
Speakeasy is a contact-based messenger.

**Guideline 1.2 — User-Generated Content**

We'd respectfully like to clarify the app's model, as we believe it was read as a
random/anonymous chat app, which it is not:

- Speakeasy does **not** connect users with random or anonymous strangers. There
  is no matchmaking. A user can only message or call someone they have
  **deliberately added by that person's handle** (or via a QR code/invite link
  the other person shared). This is the same model as a contact-based messenger.

We have also reviewed our app against each requirement in Guideline 1.2 and have
the following in place:

1. **Identifiable information + accept/decline before connecting** — Because
   connections are contact-based, you initiate a conversation only with a handle
   you have chosen to add. For calls, the incoming-call screen displays the
   caller's handle and lets the user **accept or decline** before any media
   connects.

2. **EULA with a no-tolerance policy** — Users must agree to our Terms of Use to
   use the Service; agreement is presented on the first onboarding screen, which
   states that Speakeasy has zero tolerance for harassment or objectionable
   content and links to the full Terms. The Terms are published at
   https://speakeasyapp.xyz/terms and explicitly prohibit objectionable content
   and abusive behavior.

3. **A mechanism to flag/report objectionable content** — Every conversation
   includes a Report action that files an abuse report against the user.

4. **A mechanism to block abusive users** — Every conversation includes a Block
   action; blocking stops that user's messages and calls.

5. **Filtering objectionable content** — Speakeasy is end-to-end encrypted, so we
   do not monitor private conversations. When a user reports another user, the
   report and any included content are sent to us for review and action.

6. **Acting on reports within 24 hours** — Our published Terms commit us to
   reviewing credible reports and removing offending content and ejecting the
   responsible account within 24 hours, and we have the operational process to
   do so.

We're happy to provide a demo account or a screen recording of the block,
report, and accept/decline flows if that would help the review. Thank you again.
